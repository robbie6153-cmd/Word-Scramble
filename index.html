Word Scramble

Unscramble the letters before time runs out.

How to Play

Round 1 starts with 4 letters and 10 seconds.

Each round adds 1 extra letter and 5 extra seconds.

Your score for each round is the time left on the clock.

You can use Change Letters once per round, but it costs points.

Any valid word from the given letters counts.
